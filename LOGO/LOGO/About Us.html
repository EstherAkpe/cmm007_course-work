About Us

Welcome to EstacyRice web application.

My name is Esther I was born and raised in Southern part of Nigeria, now based in the UK.

I love everything about food, I love sourcing it, cooking it and eating it.

My goal is to ensure your excited about cooking and not see cooking as a chore.

You will learn a few recipes and tricks that you can incorporate into your cooking routine so you can start cooking with confidence.

EstacyRice web application is an online application that offers different preparation of Nigerian rice recipes like Jollof rice and Coconut rice.

These delicacies are some of the meals prepared in Nigeria.

This application will provide list of Group Recipes and individual Recipes and will also provide step by step cooking instruction and how it is served.

This web application will allow chef to add or edit recipes.

This web application will allow recipes seekers to view recipes.

Our lists of recipes when combined in a right portion will you that unique taste, aroma and flavor you have always long for.
