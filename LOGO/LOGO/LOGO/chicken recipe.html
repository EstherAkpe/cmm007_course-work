Chicken Recipes

Ingredients For Fried Chicken

  • Chicken.

  • Oil of Your Choice.

  • Salt.

  • Onions of Your Choice.

  • Garlic, Fresh or Grounded.

  • Ginger, Fresh or Grounded.

  • Rosemary Powder.

  • Thyme.

  • Fresh Ppper.

  • Maggi

  • Curry Powder

  • Nutmeg

How to Prepare Fried Chicken

  • Cut the Chicken into your desire size.

      • Wash it with a clean water and put them in a pot.

      • Marinate the Chicken with seasoning and spices and leave it for minimuum 2 hours.

      • Put the Marinaade Chicken in a deep Fryer and set the timing to 10minutes,
        or Heat the oil over medium heat in a large frying pan and add the Chicken.
        make sure to turn them before they burn and cook until golden on each side.

How it is Serve

  • Serve warm.
